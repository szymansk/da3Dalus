## Wing Configuration
:stem: latexmath

image::../images/images with text/Folie1.png[]

The +WingConfiguration+ is important for the generation of an aeroplane. In general it consists of an ordered list of segments. The list always starts with one _root_-segment, followed by a number of _segment_s wich are followed by a number of _tip_-segments.

For segments of type _segment_ or _tip_ the root airfoil and chord is equal to the previous segment's tip airfoil and chord. The dihedral and the incidence angle are always 0 for the root airfoil of a segment or tip.

[plantuml,"wing_setup",svg]
.A wing consists of segments of +WingSegmentType+ _root_, _segment_ and _tip_.
----
@startebnf
WingConfiguration = root, {segment}, {tip};
@endebnf
----

=== Airfoil
[quote, Wikipedia]
____
An airfoil (American English) or aerofoil (British English) is a streamlined body that is capable of generating significantly more lift than drag.
____
The _airfoil_ (path to file) we use are in Selig-file format which describes a curve as a list of points (x,y) running from the _trailing edge_ (x=1.0) over the _upper surface_ to the _nose point_ (x=0.0) at the _leading edge_ back to the _trailing edges_ via the _lower surface_. Which is then interpolated by cadquery's https://cadquery.readthedocs.io/en/latest/classreference.html?highlight=interpolate#cadquery.Workplane.splineApprox[+splineApprox+]-function. To support cadquery in performing the loft in a ruled way, we have to make sure that the _number of interpolation points_ is equal in both airfoils that are used for the loft.

Before lofting the airfoils they can be rotated around the _chord line_ which leads to a dihedral of the wing. Or it can be rotated around a _rotation point (defined) relative (to the) chord (length)_ (latexmath:[r_{i}]). See figures xref:airfoil_parameter[xrefstyle=short], xref:airfoil_rot0[xrefstyle=short] and xref:airfoil_rot03[xrefstyle=short].

The root coordinate system latexmath:[H^N] for a segments N is calculated with the following homogeneous transformation matrixes:

latexmath:[H^0 = T(-chord_{root} r_{root}, 0,0)\cdot R_x(dihedral_{root})\cdot R_y(incidence_{root}) \cdot T(chord_{root} r_{root}, 0,0)]

latexmath:[H^i = T(-chord_{i} r_{i}, 0, 0)\cdot T(sweep_i, length_i,0) \cdot R_x(dihedral_i) \cdot R_y(incidence_i) \cdot T(chord_{i} r_{i}, 0,0)]

latexmath:[H^N = H^0\cdot\prod^N_{n=1}H^n]
