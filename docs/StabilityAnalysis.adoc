== Aerodynamics
:stem: latexmath

|===
|Header 1 |Header 2

|u      | forward (u' velocity)
|\alpha | pitch, incidence or angle of attack [\degree]
|\beta  | sideslip [\degree]

|X      | forward force [N]
|Y      | side force (positive left) [N]
|Z      | down force [N]

|L      | roll moment
|M      | pitch moment
|N      | yaw moment

|p      | roll rate (right wing down)
|q      | pitch rate (nose up)
|r      | yaw rate (nose right)

|===


== Stability Analysis

[cols="1,3,1,2,3", options="header"]
|===
|Derivative |Description |Stability Constraint |Contributes |Notes

5+|Total

|latexmath:[C_L]
|Lift Coefficient
|
|
|Dimensionless parameter characterizing the lifting capability of aircraft geometry

|latexmath:[C_D]
|Drag Coefficient
|
|
|Dimensionless parameter characterizing the aircraft geometry's total drag

5+|Drag Force

|latexmath:[C_{Xu}]
|Change in drag (or axial force) coefficient due to forward velocity (latexmath:[u'])
|speed damping (<0)
|
|Change in drag due to forward speed, indicating aerodynamic drag behavior

|latexmath:[C_{X\alpha}]
|Change in drag (or axial force) coefficient due to angle of attack (latexmath:[\alpha'])
|
|
|Indicates that drag increases with latexmath:[\alpha]

|latexmath:[C_{Xq}]
|Change in drag (or axial force) coefficient due to pitch rate (latexmath:[q'])
|
|
|Increase in drag due to aircraft pitching up or down

5+|Lift Force

|latexmath:[C_{Lu}]
|Change in lift coefficient due to forward velocity (latexmath:[u'])
|latexmath:[\Delta]Lift Coeff. due to speed
|Wings (Compressibility, Aeroelasticity)
|Indicates change in lift due to change in forward velocity (dependent on compressibility)

|latexmath:[C_{L\alpha}]
|Change in lift coefficient due to angle of attack (latexmath:[\alpha'])
|
|
|Lift generated due to changes in angle of attack, primary driver of lift

|latexmath:[C_{Lq}]
|Change in lift coefficient due to pitch rate (latexmath:[q'])
|
|
|Lift generated due to the rate of pitching

5+|Side Force

|latexmath:[C_{Y\beta}]
|Change in side force coefficient due to sideslip angle (latexmath:[\beta'])
|latexmath:[C_{Y\beta}<0]
|V-Tail, Wing Sweep (Twist and Taper)
|Side force generated by sideslip, contributes  lateral-directional stability (weathervane response)

|latexmath:[C_{Yp}]
|Change in side force coefficient due to roll rate (latexmath:[p'])
|latexmath:[C_{Yp}<0]
|Wings (Dihedral, Aspect Ratio)
|Side force on aircraft due to rolling rate, typically small

|latexmath:[C_{Yr}]
|Change in side force coefficient due to yaw rate (latexmath:[r'])
|latexmath:[C_{Yr}>0]
|V-Tail, Wings (analogues to latexmath:[C_{Zq}])
|Side force generated due to yaw rate. Yawing right (positive latexmath:[r']) results in a side force to the right as the left wing experiences more drag, pushing the aircraft in the direction of the yaw.

5+|Rolling Moment

|latexmath:[C_{lp}]
|Change in rolling moment coefficient due to roll rate (latexmath:[p'])
|latexmath:[C_{lp}<0] (ROLL DAMPING)
|Wings (decreases with wing taper)
|Stabilizing moment resisting rolling motion, can be extrapolated to spin initiation

|latexmath:[C_{l\beta}]
|Change in rolling moment coefficient due to sideslip angle (latexmath:[\beta'])
|latexmath:[C_{l\beta}<0] (DIHEDRAL EFFECT)
|Wings
|Adverse rolling moment generated due to sideslip, causing aircraft to roll in the direction of the sideslip (dihedral effect)/ most important for longitudinal stability

|latexmath:[C_{lr}]
|latexmath:[\Delta] Change in rolling moment coefficient due to yaw rate (latexmath:[r'])
|latexmath:[C_{lr}<0]
|Wings
|Rolling moment due to yaw rate, can induce yaw-roll coupling, especially in high yaw rates. Important for Dutch roll dynamics.

5+|Pitching Moment

|latexmath:[C_{mu}]
|latexmath:[\Delta] Change in pitching moment coefficient due to changes in forward velocity
|latexmath:[C_{mu} \approx 0]
|Wings (Compressiblity, Aeroelasticity)
|Indicates change in pitching moment due to change in forward velocity (dependent on compressibility)

|latexmath:[C_{m\alpha}]
|latexmath:[\Delta] Change in pitching moment coefficient due to angle of attack (latexmath:[\alpha'])
|latexmath:[C_{m\alpha}<0]
|
|Pitching moment response to alpha changes, critical in long. static stability. Determines aircraft stability margin

|latexmath:[C_{mq}]
|Change in pitching moment coefficient due to pitch rate (latexmath:[q'])
|latexmath:[C_{mq}<0] (PITCH DAMPING)
|H-Tail, Wing sweep
|Damping moment that resists further changes in pitch rate, critical for preventing pitch oscillations

5+|Yawing Moment

|latexmath:[C_{n\beta}]
|Change in yawing moment coefficient due to sideslip (latexmath:[\beta'])
|latexmath:[C_{n\beta}>0] (Weathercock Stability)
|V-Tail
|Restoring yawing moment due to sideslip, responsible for directional stability and counteracting yaw deviations

|latexmath:[C_{np}]
|latexmath:[\Delta] Change in yawing moment coefficient due to roll rate (latexmath:[p'])
|latexmath:[C_{np}<0] (not strict can be positive roll induces yaw in the same direction)
|Wings
|Yawing tendency during rolling motion

|latexmath:[C_{nr}]
|Change in yawing moment coefficient due to yaw rate (latexmath:[r']) (YAW DAMPING)
|latexmath:[C_{nr}<0] (YAW DAMPING)
|V-Tail, Wings
|Yaw damping moment resisting the rate of yaw. Provides dynamic stability by damping yaw oscillations.

|===
